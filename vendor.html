Wilson Apiaries

Stall # B11 B12

Wilson Apiaries produces local unpasteurized honey, homemade skin creams and fresh cut flowers. We are third generation beekeepers who produce local and unpasteurized honey. We currently have over 60 hives located throughout the Belleville and surrounding area. We use the beeswax from our honey production to produce skin creams. Our flower farm is located in Thomasburg, 25 minutes north of Belleville. Throughout the summer season we grow a wide variety of annuals and perennials specifically for fresh cut flowers and dried flowers. We do not use any herbicides, pesticides or fungicides on our flowers. At market we offer a wide variety of flower arrangements for in the home or a special occasion.

Lewa Farm Ontario

Stall # A31 A32

Lewa Farm is located north-east of Belleville. We exercise crop rotation, we apply organic best practices to grow diverse vegetables while maintaining our farm’s soil healthy. We do not use any type of pesticides, herbicides, nor plant any GMO seeds, encouraging a diverse ecosystem around our fields. We carefully select produce varieties that grow well and taste great. We market fresh, healthy, local food in the surrounding communities. We deliver naturally grown vegetables fresh from out fields to your doorsteps and farmers market. We consider our customers to be part of our extended family and invite you to join us as we strive to build healthier communities and environment.
