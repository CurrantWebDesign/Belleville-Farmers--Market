Stall # 11B 12B

We grow an exciting range of vegetables & herbs including several varieties of garlic, every year we introduce new vegetable varieties and other products to our market stand. All our produce is grown on our 75 acre farm just south of Napanee, close to The Bay of Quinte.

Lewa Farm Ontario

Stall # 11B 12B

Lewa Farm is located north-east of Belleville. We exercise crop rotation, we apply organic best practices to grow diverse vegetables while maintaining our farm’s soil healthy. We do not use any type of pesticides, herbicides, nor plant any GMO seeds, encouraging a diverse ecosystem around our fields. We carefully select produce varieties that grow well and taste great. We market fresh, healthy, local food in the surrounding communities. We deliver naturally grown vegetables fresh from out fields to your doorsteps and farmers market. We consider our customers to be part of our extended family and invite you to join us as we strive to build healthier communities and environment.
